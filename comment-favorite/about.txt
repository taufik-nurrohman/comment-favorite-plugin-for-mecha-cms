====

> Give a special mark on any comments that you consider important or interesting.

On the [comments manager]({{url.manager}}comment) page, edit the comment you want to mark it as a favorite. Once you get into the comment editor, click on the **Fields** tab, then check the custom field that reads _Mark as Favorite_.
